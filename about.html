Typing is more than just hitting keys.


About my team

We are a small team of developers and design enthusiasts who obsess over mechanical keyboards and WPM (Words Per Minute) charts. We built NoobDev because we wanted a better place to practice, and we hope you enjoy using it as much as we enjoyed building it.

Our Mission

At NoobDev, we believe that typing is the foundational skill of the digital age. Whether you are a coder, a writer, a student, or a gamer, the keyboard is your primary tool for expression. Our mission is simple: to help you bridge the gap between your thoughts and the screen with speed, accuracy, and fluidity.

Why We Built This

We were tired of cluttered, ad-heavy typing tutors that felt like they were stuck in the 1990s. We wanted to build a sanctuary for typists—a place that is:

  • Minimalist: No distractions. Just you and the text.
  • Customizable: Tailored to your aesthetic and performance needs.
  • Focus: A clean interface designed to help you enter the "flow state."
